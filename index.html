Sortie 2035

在不久的将来，人类对海域的探索更进一步，海底牧场的建设趋于完善，娱乐活动也向其中延伸。Sortie是一款基于海底牧场场景的单人移动载具，协助用户进行一小时左右的浅海探索，并为其提供采摘的基础设备。

Accordion

Accordion’s goal is to help customers manage their underwear or any kinds of lingerie products. Small sized clothing usually are the last thing we will consider when comes to managing a wardrobe.

Velcro tapes allows “Accordion” can hang or stick to different platforms. the fabric we will be using for the main body is the waterproof nylon cotton fabric that will increase the sustainability of our product.
